## 🚀 6 Powerful Modules

### 🤖 1. Hyper-Expanded RAG Bot
Ask anything about jobs, APIs, HR scripts, 
off-campus tricks, interview prep — get 
instant AI-powered answers.

### 🏢 2. Advanced Company Matrix
Enter your CGPA → instantly see which 
companies (TCS, Infosys, Amazon, Microsoft, 
Adobe, Flipkart & 15+ more) you're eligible 
for, with package range & required skills.

### 🗺️ 3. Role-Based API Roadmaps
Choose your dream role (AI/ML Engineer, 
Full-Stack, DevOps, UI/UX, Business Analyst) 
+ target company → get a month-by-month 
learning roadmap.

### 🎓 4. Smart Internship Navigator
Semester-wise internship strategy — PPO 
guidance, stipend ranges, Wellfound & 
hackathon pathways.

### 📄 5. AI ATS Resume Evaluator
Upload your resume (PDF/DOCX) → get ATS 
score, keyword density analysis, and 
structural improvement suggestions.

### 🎯 6. AI Live Mock Test & HR Simulator
Simulate real interview rounds — General HR, 
Core Python & API Coding, DBMS & SQL Query 
Optimization with live evaluation.
